### Ingredients

* ½ cup quinoa
* 1½ cup water
* 1 apple peeled cored and diced
* honey
* cinnamon

> **Pro Tip:** 1½ times water for the quinoa

### Cooking Instructions

1. Heat in a saucepan until all the water is gone
2. Add cinnamon and honey to taste
3. Can for later
