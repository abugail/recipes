### Ingredients

* 1 jar of beef or chicken bouillon granules
* 2½ cups plain flour

### Instructions

1. Mix together and store in jar.

> **Pro Tip:** When cooking gravy, follow below

### Ingredients

* ¼ cup of mix
* 1½ cups water

### Instructions

1. Combine and stir over medium  heat  until thickened

* Yields 2½ cups gravy
