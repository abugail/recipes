Sprout n' Spinach Salad

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
5 mins 0 mins 5 mins
Serves 1

Ingredients

  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 1 handful alfalfa sprouts
  • ½ orange bell pepper, diced
  • ½ carrot, grated
  • 2 tbs hummus
  • 1 tbs olive oil

Pro Tip: Mix hummus and olive oil seperately before placing in salad
