## Strawberry Spinach Salad

| Prep Time  | Cook Time    | Total Time  |
| ---------- |:------------:| -----------:|
| 5 mins    | 0 mins      | 5 mins     |


###### Serves 1

![Not Loading](http://i.imgur.com/cZg4jKbl.png)

### Ingredients

* 1 cup spinach
* 1 cup strawberries
* 1 thick slice fresh mozzarella cheese
* 2 tbs pine nuts
* 1 cup broccoli
* 1 tbs olive oil
* 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
* 1 squeeze lemon juice
