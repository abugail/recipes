Healthy Tortillas ★★★★

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
? mins ? mins ? mins
Makes 8
Inspired by: Eat2Live GTA

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup arrowroot
  • 1 tbs coconut flour
  • 1 tbs water
  • pinch of salt

Pro Tip: cook over VERY low heat so as not to burn

Cooking Instructions

  1. Combine ALL ingredients into food processor
  2. Pour into low heat frying pan
